# 如何订购 KIGLAND 产品 / How to Order

KIGLAND 目前提供标准款和定制款两类订购方式。你可以根据自己是否已经有明确角色、是否刚好喜欢我们已有的款式，以及预算和制作需求，选择更适合自己的产品。

---

## 1. 如果你喜欢的角色，我们刚好做了

如果你已经有喜欢的角色，而 KIGLAND 恰好推出了相关款式，比如我们二次创作的"初音未来""岛风""流萤""莹"等角色风格产品，并且刚好符合你的口味，那么 **Type-M** 通常是最合适、也最实惠的选择。

Type-M 是 KIGLAND 的标准化产品系列，价格更低，购买流程更简单，也更适合第一次尝试 Kigurumi 的玩家。你可以直接选择已有款式，下单后按照商品说明等待发货。

这类产品也适合喜欢 DIY 的玩家。收到头壳后，你可以根据自己的 Cosplay 需求进行搭配或修改，例如更换服装、调整配件、重新搭配假发、增加小装饰，或进一步做个性化改造。对于想用较低预算进入 Kigurumi 体验的人来说，Type-M 是很好的起点。

## 2. 如果你想做指定角色

如果你已经有喜欢的角色，但 KIGLAND 目前还没有推出对应款式，就需要先联系我们进行定制咨询。客服会根据角色造型、制作难度、还原要求和当前排单情况，为你判断是否适合制作，并给出对应报价。

为了让我们更准确地评估，请尽量提供清晰的角色参考图。最理想的参考图是角色的正视图，也就是类似"一寸照片"那样，脸部朝前、五官清楚、发型无遮挡、光线均匀的图片。这样的图片能帮助我们更好地判断脸型、眼睛、刘海、发色和整体风格。

如果你有侧面图、三视图、表情图、设定图或你喜欢的其他参考案例，也可以一起提供。资料越完整，沟通和评估会越准确。原创角色、二创角色、游戏角色、动画角色等都可以先咨询，最终是否承接以客服评估为准。

## 3. 订购流程大致是怎样的？

以下流程仅作为参考，具体步骤会根据产品类型、客服沟通和实际制作安排调整。

1. **咨询与提交资料**
   你可以先联系 KIGLAND 客服，说明想要购买 Type-M 标准款，或想要定制某个角色。如果是定制产品，请同时提供角色参考图和你的主要需求。

2. **评估与报价**
   我们会根据角色复杂度、制作范围、材料需求和当前排单情况进行评估，并给出初步报价和预计工期。部分复杂角色可能需要进一步沟通后才能确认。

3. **完成测量工作**
   如果产品涉及紧身衣、佩戴尺寸或其他需要匹配身体数据的部分，我们会提供测量指引。你需要按照要求完成测量，并将数据提交给客服。测量数据会影响最终穿戴效果，请尽量认真填写。

4. **拍下定金**
   确认报价、制作方向和测量信息后，你需要按照客服指引拍下定金。定制产品在进入制作流程后，定金通常不支持退还，请在付款前确认需求、价格和周期。

5. **确认收款并进入设计**
   我们确认收到定金后，会开始进入设计和制作准备流程。根据产品类型不同，可能会先进行 3D 设计、结构判断、发型拆分或颜色方案确认。

6. **确认 3D 设计图**
   在设计阶段，我们会提供 3D 设计图或阶段性设计效果，用于和你确认角色脸型、整体比例、头发结构等关键内容。此阶段请认真检查，并及时反馈需要调整的地方。

7. **确认头发颜色与细节**
   制作前或制作过程中，我们会与你确认头发颜色、整体色调和部分外观细节。由于屏幕显示、材料和灯光环境不同，实物颜色可能会存在一定差异。

8. **等待制作效果图**
   产品制作完成或接近完成时，我们会根据实际情况提供制作效果图，让你确认整体效果。不同产品类型的确认节点可能不同，具体以客服沟通为准。

9. **确认后发货交付**
   当效果确认完成，并且尾款、物流等事项处理完毕后，我们会安排发货，将产品交付给你。收到产品后，请先检查包装、面单、头壳本体和配件是否完整。

## 4. 下单前的小提醒

定制产品和普通现货商品不同，会涉及角色理解、3D 设计、材料制作、手工处理和装配调试。为了获得更顺利的订购体验，建议你在下单前准备好清晰参考图，并提前想清楚自己最在意的部分，例如眼睛、脸型、发色、可爱感、还原度或整体气质。

如果你还不确定适合 Type-M、Type-C 还是 Type-G，也可以先联系客服。我们会根据你的预算、角色需求和使用场景，帮你判断更合适的产品类型。

## How to Order

KIGLAND offers two types of ordering: standard models and custom builds. Which one you choose depends on whether you already have a specific character in mind, whether you like one of our existing designs, and your budget and requirements.

### 1. If we already made the character you love

If you have a favorite character and KIGLAND happens to have a model that matches — for example, our takes on styles inspired by characters like Hatsune Miku, Shimakaze, or similar — then **Type-M** is usually the best and most affordable choice.

Type-M is our standardized product line: lower price, simpler ordering, and great for first-timers. Pick an existing design, place your order, and wait for delivery.

Type-M also works well for DIY enthusiasts. After receiving the headshell, you can customize it further — swap the outfit, change accessories, re-style the wig, add small decorations, or make your own modifications. It's an excellent entry point for anyone who wants to try Kigurumi on a budget.

### 2. If you want a specific character (custom)

If you have a character in mind but we don't currently have a matching model, you'll need to contact us for a custom consultation. Our team will evaluate based on the character's design, production complexity, accuracy requirements, and current queue status.

To help us assess accurately, please provide clear character reference images. The ideal reference is a front-facing portrait — like a passport photo — with the face centered, features clearly visible, no hair obstructing the face, and even lighting. This helps us judge the face shape, eyes, bangs, hair color, and overall style.

Side views, turnaround sheets, expression references, concept art, or any other reference material you like are also welcome. The more complete your materials, the more accurate our evaluation. Original characters, fan designs, game characters, and anime characters are all acceptable — final acceptance depends on our team's assessment.

### 3. What does the ordering process look like?

The following is a general reference. Actual steps may vary depending on product type and specific arrangements.

1. **Consultation & submit materials**
   Contact KIGLAND customer service. Let us know if you want a Type-M standard model or a custom build. For customs, include your character references and key requirements.

2. **Evaluation & quote**
   We'll assess complexity, scope, materials, and our current production queue, then provide a preliminary quote and estimated timeline. Complex characters may require additional discussion.

3. **Complete measurements**
   If your product involves a zentai bodysuit or requires body-matched sizing, we'll provide measurement guidance. Follow the instructions carefully — your measurements directly affect the final fit.

4. **Place deposit**
   Once the quote, direction, and measurements are confirmed, place your deposit as instructed. For custom orders, deposits are generally non-refundable once production begins. Confirm your requirements, price, and timeline before paying.

5. **Design phase begins**
   After we confirm receipt of your deposit, design and production preparation starts. Depending on the product type, this may include 3D modeling, structural planning, hair breakdown, or color scheme confirmation.

6. **Review 3D design**
   During the design phase, we'll share 3D design drafts or progress renders for your review — face shape, proportions, hair structure, and other key elements. Check carefully and let us know if anything needs adjustment.

7. **Confirm hair color & details**
   Before or during production, we'll confirm hair color, overall tones, and certain appearance details. Actual colors may differ slightly from screen previews due to display, material, and lighting variations.

8. **Wait for production preview**
   When the product is complete or near completion, we'll share production preview images for your approval. Confirmation checkpoints vary by product type.

9. **Final delivery**
   Once everything is confirmed and payment is settled, we'll ship your product. Upon receiving it, check the packaging, labels, headshell, and accessories for completeness and any issues.

### 4. A few things to know before ordering

Custom kigurumi products aren't like buying something off the shelf. They involve character interpretation, 3D design, material fabrication, handcraft work, and assembly. For the smoothest experience, prepare clear reference images beforehand and think about what matters most to you — the eyes, the face shape, the hair color, the overall cuteness, accuracy, or general vibe.

If you're unsure whether Type-M, Type-C, or Type-G is right for you, reach out to us. We'll help you figure out the best fit based on your budget, character needs, and how you plan to use the headshell.
