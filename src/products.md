# KIGLAND 产品系列介绍 / Product Lineup

KIGLAND 的产品系列使用 **M.A.G.I.C** 作为整体命名体系。它不是某一个单独产品的名称，而是 KIGLAND 对不同产品线的统一归类方式，类似 Tesla 用 `S / 3 / X / Y` 组成完整产品矩阵。每一个字母都对应一种产品方向，用来区分不同阶段、不同定位、不同交付方式的 Kigurumi 头壳产品。

目前，KIGLAND 面向用户主要开放的是 **Type-M、Type-C、Type-G** 三类产品。除此之外，**Type-A**（也称 **Alpha**）是 KIGLAND 开店初期的初代探索产品；**Type-I**（Industrial，工业化方向）由于供应链和制造体系仍在建设中，暂未作为正式对外产品启用。

> 👉 不确定怎么选？先看 [如何订购 / How to Order](./how-to-order.md)，或直接联系客服。

## 1. M.A.G.I.C：KIGLAND 的产品谱系

M.A.G.I.C 代表 KIGLAND 从早期探索、标准化量产、角色定制，到未来工业化生产的完整产品路径。不同 Type 对应不同用户需求：有的适合第一次尝试 Kigurumi 的玩家，有的适合明确角色设定的定制需求，有的面向更复杂的高阶制作，也有的保留给未来更大规模的制造形态。

因此在帮助中心里，M.A.G.I.C 更适合作为"产品系列总览"，而不是和 Type-M、Type-C、Type-G 并列的某个产品。用户真正下单时，需要选择的是具体 Type。

## 2. Type-M：标准化平价产品

Type-M 是 KIGLAND 当前面向大众玩家推出的标准化平价产品。它采用固定款式和相对标准化的生产方式，用户可以根据肩宽、佩戴需求等信息选择合适规格。

Type-M 适合第一次尝试 Kigurumi 的玩家，也适合希望以更友好的预算获得完整角色扮演体验的用户。相比传统 5,000 元到 10,000 元区间的高价定制头壳，Type-M 的目标是降低 Kigurumi 的入门门槛，让更多玩家可以更轻松地拥有自己的角色形象。

## 3. Type-C：角色定制产品

Type-C 是 KIGLAND 当前面向用户开放的角色定制产品系列，适合已经有明确角色设定、希望制作专属头壳的玩家。

与 Type-M 的固定款式不同，Type-C 会根据用户提供的角色资料进行建模与制作，包括角色脸型、眼睛、表情、发型适配和整体风格等内容。用户可以提交角色设定图、三视图、参考图或原创角色资料，KIGLAND 会根据资料完整度、造型复杂度和制作可行性进行评估。下单前需要先与客服确认制作范围、价格、周期和付款方式。

## 4. Type-G：高阶定制产品

Type-G 面向更高要求、更复杂角色需求的定制用户。它适合对还原度、结构、造型或整体表现有更高要求的玩家，也适合部分展演、品牌合作、商业项目或特殊角色制作需求。

Type-G 通常会涉及更多非标准设计，例如复杂发型、特殊脸型、异形结构、特殊材质表现，或更完整的角色外观方案。它的制作自由度更高，评估和制作流程也更复杂。具体价格、工期和制作范围，需要在提交角色资料后由客服单独评估。

## 5. Type-A / Alpha：初代探索产品

Type-A（Alpha）是 KIGLAND 开店之初的初代产品，代表 KIGLAND 最早期对 Kigurumi 头壳产品的理解和尝试，包括基础结构、外观风格、佩戴方式和交付流程的早期版本。

Alpha 的意义在于记录品牌起点：它帮助 KIGLAND 完成了从"可以制作"到"可以交付"的第一轮验证，也积累了最早一批用户反馈。随着产品结构、生产流程和审美标准不断迭代，Type-A 已不再是当前主要推荐的产品系列。

## 6. Type-I：工业化产品方向，暂未启用

Type-I 中的 I 代表 Industrial，即工业化方向，指向 KIGLAND 未来更高规模、更稳定供应链、更完整制造体系下的产品形态。

目前 KIGLAND 仍在持续建设生产系统、供应链能力和交付流程，尚未彻底进入"超级工厂"阶段，因此 Type-I 暂时不作为正式对外产品启用。

## KIGLAND Product Lineup

KIGLAND organizes its products under the **M.A.G.I.C** naming system — a unified framework that categorizes different product lines by stage, positioning, and delivery method. Much like how Tesla uses `S / 3 / X / Y` to structure its vehicle lineup, each letter in M.A.G.I.C corresponds to a distinct product direction.

Currently, KIGLAND offers **Type-M, Type-C, and Type-G** to customers. **Type-A** (Alpha) was our earliest product generation, and **Type-I** (Industrial) represents a future direction that is not yet publicly available.

### 1. M.A.G.I.C: The Product Framework

M.A.G.I.C maps KIGLAND's journey from early experimentation to standardized production, custom orders, and future-scale manufacturing. Each Type serves a different user: some are ideal for first-timers, some for fans with specific character requests, some for advanced or commercial needs, and some reserved for large-scale industrial production down the road.

When ordering, you choose a specific Type — M.A.G.I.C is the organizing framework, not a product itself.

### 2. Type-M: Standardized, Affordable

Type-M is KIGLAND's standardized product line built for accessibility. It uses fixed models and relatively standardized production, with sizing matched to shoulder width and wear requirements.

Type-M is made for first-time kigurumi players and anyone who wants a complete character experience without the ¥5,000–¥10,000 price tag of traditional custom headshells. The goal is simple: lower the barrier so more people can own their character.

### 3. Type-C: Character Custom

Type-C is KIGLAND's character-specific custom product line. It's for players who already have a defined character and want a headshell made specifically for that design.

Unlike Type-M's fixed models, Type-C involves modeling and production based on your character references — face shape, eyes, expression, hairstyle, and overall aesthetic. You submit character art, reference images, or original character designs, and we evaluate based on complexity, completeness, and production feasibility. Before ordering, you'll confirm scope, pricing, timeline, and payment with our team.

### 4. Type-G: Advanced Custom

Type-G is for high-spec, high-complexity custom projects. This includes users with demanding accuracy requirements, complex character designs, professional exhibitions, brand collaborations, commercial projects, or special character builds.

Type-G often involves non-standard elements — complex hairstyles, unusual face shapes, non-standard structures, special materials, or full character appearance solutions. Pricing, timeline, and scope are evaluated case-by-case after submitting character reference materials.

### 5. Type-A / Alpha: The Original

Type-A (Alpha) is KIGLAND's first-generation product from our earliest days. It represents our initial understanding of kigurumi headshells — the basics of structure, style, fit, and delivery.

Alpha matters because it took us from "we can make this" to "we can deliver this" and gave us our first batch of real user feedback. As our production and standards have evolved, Type-A is no longer a primary recommendation, but it's where we started.

### 6. Type-I: Industrial (Not Yet Available)

The I in Type-I stands for Industrial. It represents KIGLAND's vision for higher-scale production with a more mature supply chain and manufacturing system.

We're still building toward that stage, so Type-I is not currently available as an external product. It's kept in our internal roadmap but marked as "Coming Soon" in user-facing materials.
